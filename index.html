What is RW?

RW Clan

RW is a competitive clan in the browser-based multiplayer archery game Narrow.one. Built around skill, trust, and coordination, we are a community of players who push each other to the highest level the game has to offer.

"Fight together. Win together."

Under the leadership of NubitA, RW maintains a strict but fair entry process to ensure every member is genuinely committed to the team. If you're ready to prove yourself, the gates are open.

Recruitment

How to Join RW

This test is designed to select individuals who possess the skills and reliability befitting an RW member. Open to anyone — prove yourself across all four stages.

1
Stage 1
Fruit
Take a photo of the fruit at the gatehouse and ping @examiner to report it with the photo.
CN / NV members may skip this stage
2
Stage 2 · TA
Speed Flag Capture
Capture the flag in the Arena within 13 seconds.
Submit a video to the examiner, or have the examiner accompany you in-game.
CN / NV members may skip this stage
3
Stage 3
Skill Assessment — 1v1
Win a first-to-10 match versus an @examiner.
Maps: Castle or Arena  |  Bow: Any  |  Regeneration limit: 12
4
Stage 4
Trustworthiness Assessment
Internal review based on rule violations, communication, and sense of responsibility.
Pass condition: 60% approval from current members.
⚔ Rules After Admission
  • Violation of rules or loss of trust may result in a ban after leader and sub-leader review.
  • Rules may be revised depending on clan status.
Ready to apply? Head to our Discord server and ask for the @examiner role to begin the test process.

RW Mods

Beyond competing, RW builds tools that elevate the Narrow.one experience for everyone. These mods are crafted by RW members — exclusively available to the clan.

👁
Spectator MOD
OBSERVATION

Watches all players from a third-person perspective and automatically follows them in real time. Perfect for scouting, coaching, or simply enjoying the action from any angle on the field.

☠
Kill Log MOD
REAL-TIME

Displays a live feed of every kill as it happens — who eliminated who, instantly. Stay on top of the battlefield without missing a single elimination.

⬡
Ally Position MOD
TEAMPLAY

Renders each teammate's avatar and exact distance directly on your screen. Always know where your wolves are — coordinate pushes and retreats with precision.

◈
UI MOD
COSMETIC

A complete UI reskin built around RW's identity — deep black as the base with sharp red accents for a clean, focused aesthetic that looks as sharp as you play.
